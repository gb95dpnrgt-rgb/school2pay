# School2Pay

School2Pay is a B2B SaaS platform for UK schools to collect payments from parents for trips, clubs, dinners, and uniforms (not tuition fees). A school administrator creates payment requests and assigns them to students; parents receive a signed magic link by email and pay instantly without creating an account. Schools are organised under legal trusts, which are Stripe Connect connected accounts and act as the merchant of record — parent money flows directly to the school, and School2Pay takes a small flat application fee per transaction without ever holding funds.
